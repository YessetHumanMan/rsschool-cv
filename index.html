About Me

Welcome everyone to my website! My name is Yesset, I'm from Astana. I've been doing front-end development for over a year. Before development, I was doing something I didn’t like, I went to a job I didn’t like, I couldn’t figure out what to do to get things done brought pleasure and money, until I came across an interesting development school.

Since then I have gone through difficult times. Studying was given in different ways: in some places it was easier, in others it was more difficult. The daily work on my skills and the diligence with which I approached learning, after just a few months bore fruit - I began to understand programming and write code better.

Dear reader, if you are reading this, know that behind every difficult time comes relief, the most important thing is not to give up!
